Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about GiveGrip

GiveGrip is a crowdfunding platform that connects donors with meaningful causes. Campaign creators can set up fundraising campaigns, and donors can contribute securely through our platform. We handle all the payment processing and ensure transparency throughout the process.

Yes, absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your personal and financial information. All payments are processed through secure payment gateways, and we never store your credit card information on our servers.

We charge a small platform fee of 5% on successful campaigns to cover our operational costs. Payment processing fees (typically 2.9% + $0.30) are also applied by our payment partners. The majority of your donation goes directly to the cause.

We have a thorough verification process for all campaigns. This includes identity verification, document review, and background checks. We also monitor campaigns for suspicious activity and have a reporting system for users to flag concerns.

Refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you made a donation in error or have concerns about a campaign, please contact our support team within 7 days of your donation. We'll work with you to resolve the issue.

Tax deductibility depends on the specific campaign and organization. Many campaigns are run by registered 501(c)(3) nonprofits, which are tax deductible. You'll receive a receipt for your donation that you can use for tax purposes.
