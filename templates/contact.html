Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I make a donation?

Making a donation is easy! Simply browse our campaigns, choose one you'd like to support, click "Donate Now," and follow the secure payment process.

Is my donation secure?

Absolutely! We use bank-level security with SSL encryption and PCI compliance. Your financial information is never stored on our servers.

Can I make anonymous donations?

Yes, you can choose to make anonymous donations. Your name won't be displayed publicly, but we'll still send you a receipt for tax purposes.

How do I get a receipt for my donation?

You'll receive an email receipt immediately after your donation is processed. You can also access all your donation receipts in your account dashboard.
